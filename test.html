Sno. Observation Requirement Recommendation
1. Non - Compliance with Independence Requirements by auditors &/or audit firms. In order to avoid Self Review Threat, an auditor or audit firm shall not provide directly or indirectly to the company or its holding company or subsidiary company any of the services mentioned in Section 144(8) of the Companies Act 2013 Evaluate Non-Audit services closely and ensure that independence is not impaired: Following is an indicative list of non-audit services:
(a) accounting and book keeping
(b) internal audit
(c) design and implementation of any financial information system
(d) actuarial services
(e) investment advisory services
(f) investment banking services
(g) rendering of outsourced financial services
(h) management services
(i) any other kind of services as may be prescribed
